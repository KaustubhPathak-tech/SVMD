Mandiram Trust

Mandiram is a charitable trust devoted to preserving temple heritage, serving the community through education, health camps, and cultural programs, and supporting families in need.

Founded with a vision of compassion and service, Mandiram organises annual festivals, provides scholarships, and runs free medical camps reaching thousands each year.

  • Founder: Sri Raghavendra Pillai
  • Mission: Preserve temple heritage and uplift the local community
